At the Heart of Capas Lies the Spirit of the Aeta People

Deep within the mountains and fields of Capas, Tarlac, the Aeta community continues to stand strong — rooted in culture, faith, and unity. Despite the many challenges they face, the Aeta families remain hopeful and hardworking, finding strength in their traditions and the love they share for one another.

Who They Are

The Aeta of Capas are among the first stewards of the land, known for their deep connection to nature and their simple, joyful way of life. They embody resilience — adapting to change while keeping their rich heritage alive. Through every smile and story, they remind us that true strength comes from community and compassion.

Once living freely as hunter-gatherers, they now struggle with the effects of logging, mining, and land grabbing. To help them overcome these challenges, a project was created to strengthen their unity, raise awareness of their rights, and support their effort to secure their ancestral land through the Certificate of Ancestral Domain Title (CADT). The program also trains local leaders to analyze problems, make informed decisions, and represent their communities effectively. Sa paanan ng bundok at gitna ng luntiang kalikasan, matatagpuan ang mga Aeta ng Capas — mga taong may pusong malapit sa kalikasan, mayaman sa kultura, at puno ng kabutihan. Sa kabila ng mga pagsubok, patuloy silang nagsisikap para sa kinabukasan ng kanilang mga pamilya. Sa pamamagitan ng aming mga programa sa edukasyon, kabuhayan, at kalusugan, hangarin naming mas mapalakas ang kanilang kakayahan at mapangalagaan ang kanilang pamana. Ang bawat donasyon ay hindi lamang tulong — ito ay pakikiisa sa kanilang kwento at laban para sa mas maunlad na bukas. Sa simpleng ambag mo, nagiging parte ka ng pagbibigay pag-asa at pagkakataon sa mga pamilyang Aeta upang maipagmalaki ang kanilang kultura at magkaroon ng mas maayos na kinabukasan.
