DAYON AETA

What is Dayon Aeta?

Dayon Aeta is a community project that aims to support and empower the Aeta people through education, livelihood programs, and partnerships with volunteers and donors.

Where does the support or donation go?

All support directly benefits Aeta families through educational supplies, livelihood training, community projects, and other initiatives that improve their quality of life.

How can I volunteer?

You can join us by filling out our volunteer form or contacting our team. We welcome individuals and groups who want to share their time, skills, and knowledge with the Aeta community.

Can organizations or companies partner with you?

Absolutely! We encourage partnerships with organizations and businesses to expand the reach and impact of our programs. Contact us to discuss how we can collaborate.

Can I donate to support the projects?

Yes! Donations are a big help in sustaining our programs. You can donate through our official channels — just reach out to us for details on how to send your support.

How are the funds managed or tracked?

We ensure transparency by keeping records of all donations and expenditures. Regular updates are also shared with our partners and supporters.
