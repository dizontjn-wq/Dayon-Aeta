OUR PROGRAMS

EDUCATION FOR A BRIGHTER FUTURE

The photos show Aeta children joyfully participating in their lessons despite the long distances many of them walk to reach school. The program supports them by providing school supplies, learning materials, and community-based classes led by volunteers who visit remote areas. Their determination and enthusiasm for learning inspire the continued effort to give Aeta children access to quality education.

LIVELIHOOD & ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT

The photos show Aeta men and women working together in farming, crafting, and other livelihood activities that reflect their deep connection to the land. Through training, tools, and essential resources, the program helps them develop sustainable skills and improve their income. Each new skill empowers families to build long-term stability and rise above poverty with dignity.

HEALTH CARE & WELL-BEING

The photos show Aeta families patiently receiving medical assistance, with volunteers providing medicines, vitamins, and essential health services. Because many communities lack access to clinics and trained professionals, these missions offer vital check-ups, treatment, and health education. The relief and gratitude seen in the families' faces reflect the importance of ongoing health support to help them build a healthier future.

COMMUNITY PROJECTS

The photos show Aeta families and volunteers coming together in shared spaces, highlighting the unity that grows through community activities. They also capture the development of facilities like meeting areas, learning centers, and improved housing built through joint effort between the organization and the families. Each project enhances daily living and strengthens the entire community.
